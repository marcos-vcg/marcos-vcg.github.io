About Me
Here's a little about me
My name is Marcos Costa. Bachelor of Nautical Sciences, Technologist in Systems Analysis and Development, postgraduate degree in Software Engineering and completing a postgraduate degree in Information Security.
 

I have been studying software development for 8 years, in addition to working as a full stack software developer for the last 5 years. In the meantime I had the opportunity to participate in major projects such as: QuintoAndar, Smiles Viagens, Unichristus, Furukawa, in addition to having worked on several smaller projects.

 

I am passionate about systems development. I am always looking for opportunities to contribute my knowledge to new projects, and in this way, increasingly improve my technical skills and soft skills, in order to improve my performance and productivity, and thus, add more value to the projects I participate in.

 

Being challenged and staying updated are my biggest motivations.
